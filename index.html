What Is Compound Interest?

Compound interest is interest earned on both your original money and the interest that has already been added to your balance.

How to Use This Compound Interest Calculator

Enter your initial investment, monthly contribution, expected annual interest rate, number of years, and compounding frequency.

Why Monthly Contributions Matter

Monthly contributions can make a major difference over time. Even small deposits may grow significantly when combined with time and compound interest.

Compound Interest Formula

The basic compound interest formula is A = P(1 + r/n)nt.
