What Is an Investment Calculator?

An investment calculator estimates how money may grow over time based on a starting amount, regular contributions, expected return rate, and compounding frequency.

How This Calculator Works

This calculator projects investment growth by applying compound returns and adding contributions over the selected investment period.

Why Contributions Matter

Regular contributions can significantly increase long-term investment growth because each deposit has the potential to earn returns over time.

Example Calculation

If you start with $20,000, add $1,000 per month, and earn a 6% annual return for 10 years, this calculator estimates the ending balance, contributions, and interest earned.

Tips to Improve Investment Growth

  • Start investing earlier to give your money more time to compound.
  • Increase contributions when possible.
  • Compare different return rates to understand risk and reward.
  • Review fees, taxes, and inflation when making real investment decisions.
