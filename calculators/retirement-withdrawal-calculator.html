What Is a Retirement Withdrawal Calculator?

A retirement withdrawal calculator estimates how much you may be able to withdraw regularly from your retirement savings over a selected number of years.

How This Calculator Works

This calculator uses your retirement savings, expected annual return, retirement length, and desired ending balance to estimate a monthly withdrawal amount.

Why Withdrawal Planning Matters

Withdrawal planning helps estimate whether your savings may support your retirement income needs without running out too early.

Example Calculation

If you have $500,000 saved, expect a 5% annual return, and want your money to last 25 years, this calculator estimates a monthly withdrawal amount.
