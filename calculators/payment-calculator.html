What Is a Payment Calculator?

A payment calculator estimates the monthly payment required to repay a fixed-rate loan over a selected period of time.

How to Use This Payment Calculator

Enter the loan amount, loan term, and annual interest rate. The calculator will estimate your monthly payment, total payments, total interest, and yearly amortization schedule.

Why Monthly Payments Matter

Knowing your estimated monthly payment helps you compare loan options, plan your budget, and understand the long-term cost of borrowing.

Payment Formula

The fixed loan payment formula uses loan amount, monthly interest rate, and total number of payments to estimate the required monthly payment.

Example Calculation

If you borrow $200,000 for 15 years at a 6% annual interest rate, this calculator can estimate your monthly payment and total interest paid.
