What Is a Deferred Payment Loan?

A deferred payment loan is a loan where the borrower pays back the full amount at maturity instead of making regular payments during the term.

How This Calculator Works

This calculator compounds the loan balance over time and estimates the amount due at the end of the loan term.

Why Total Interest Matters

Because payments are deferred, interest may accumulate over the full term. The final amount due can be much higher than the original loan amount.

Example Calculation

If you borrow $100,000 for 10 years at 6% annual interest compounded annually, this calculator estimates the amount due at loan maturity.
