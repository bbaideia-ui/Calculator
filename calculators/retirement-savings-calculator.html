What Is a Retirement Savings Calculator?

A retirement savings calculator estimates how much you may need to save regularly to reach a target retirement amount by a planned retirement age.

How This Calculator Works

This calculator compares your current savings and future investment growth with your retirement goal, then estimates the monthly savings needed to close the gap.

Why Monthly Savings Matter

Saving consistently can make a major difference over time because contributions may grow with compound investment returns.

Example Calculation

If you are 35, plan to retire at 67, currently have $30,000 saved, and want $600,000 by retirement, this calculator estimates the monthly contribution needed.
