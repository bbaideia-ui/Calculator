What Is a Credit Card Payoff Calculator?

A credit card payoff calculator estimates how long it may take to pay off credit card balances based on your monthly budget, interest rates, and minimum payments.

How This Calculator Works

This calculator uses a debt avalanche strategy, which prioritizes extra payments toward the card with the highest interest rate while maintaining minimum payments on the others.

Why Interest Rates Matter

Credit card interest can grow quickly. Paying extra toward higher-interest balances first may reduce total interest paid over time.

Example Calculation

If you have multiple credit card balances and set aside $500 per month, this calculator can estimate your payoff timeline and total interest paid.
