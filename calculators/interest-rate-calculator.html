What Is an Interest Rate Calculator?

An interest rate calculator estimates the annual rate on a loan when you know the loan amount, monthly payment, and repayment term.

How to Use This Interest Rate Calculator

Enter the loan amount, loan term, and monthly payment. The calculator estimates the annual interest rate, total payments, and total interest paid.

Why Interest Rate Matters

The interest rate affects how much you pay over the life of a loan. Even a small difference in rate can change the total interest paid.

Example Calculation

If you borrow $32,000 for 3 years and pay $960 per month, this calculator can estimate the interest rate and total interest paid.
