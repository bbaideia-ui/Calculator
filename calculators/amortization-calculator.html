What Is an Amortization Calculator?

An amortization calculator shows how a fixed-rate loan is paid down over time through regular payments.

How to Use This Amortization Calculator

Enter the loan amount, loan term, and annual interest rate. The calculator will estimate monthly payment, total interest, total payments, and a yearly amortization schedule.

Why Amortization Matters

Amortization helps you understand how much of each payment goes toward interest and how much goes toward reducing the loan balance.

Amortization Schedule

The amortization schedule shows estimated yearly interest, principal paid, and remaining balance for the selected loan term.

Example Calculation

If you borrow $200,000 for 15 years at a 6% annual interest rate, this calculator can estimate your monthly payment and payoff schedule.
