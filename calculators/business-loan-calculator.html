What Is a Business Loan Calculator?

A business loan calculator estimates the cost of borrowing money for business purposes, including payment amount, interest, fees, and total repayment cost.

How to Use This Business Loan Calculator

Enter the loan amount, interest rate, term, and fees. The calculator will estimate monthly payment, total interest, total fees, estimated APR, and an amortization schedule.

Why Business Loan Fees Matter

Business loans may include origination fees, documentation fees, and other costs. These fees can increase the true cost of borrowing beyond the stated interest rate.

APR vs Interest Rate

The interest rate shows the cost of borrowing before fees. APR gives a broader estimate of the cost because it includes certain fees in the calculation.

Example Calculation

If a business borrows $10,000 for 5 years at a 10% interest rate with fees, this calculator can estimate monthly payment, total cost, and APR.
