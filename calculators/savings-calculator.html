What Is a Savings Calculator?

A savings calculator estimates how your money may grow over time based on deposits, contributions, interest rate, compounding, and time.

How to Use This Savings Calculator

Enter your initial deposit, regular contributions, expected interest rate, compounding frequency, years to save, and tax rate. The calculator estimates your ending balance and total interest earned.

Why Contributions Matter

Regular contributions can have a major impact on long-term savings. Even small monthly or annual deposits may grow significantly when combined with interest over time.

Savings Growth Formula

This calculator estimates growth by applying interest over time and adding scheduled contributions. Actual results may vary depending on fees, taxes, rates, and contribution timing.

Example Calculation

If you start with $20,000, contribute $5,000 annually, earn 3% interest, and save for 10 years, this calculator can estimate your future savings balance.
