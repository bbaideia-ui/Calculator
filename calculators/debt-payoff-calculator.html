What Is a Debt Payoff Calculator?

A debt payoff calculator estimates how long it may take to pay off multiple debts based on balances, interest rates, monthly payments, and extra payments.

How to Use This Debt Payoff Calculator

Enter each debt balance, monthly payment, interest rate, and any extra monthly payment. Then choose a payoff strategy to estimate your repayment schedule.

Debt Avalanche vs. Debt Snowball

The debt avalanche method focuses on debts with the highest interest rate first. The debt snowball method focuses on the smallest balance first.

Why Extra Payments Matter

Extra payments can reduce payoff time and total interest because more money goes toward lowering your remaining balances.
