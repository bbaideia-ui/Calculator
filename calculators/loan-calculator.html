What Is a Loan Calculator?

A loan calculator estimates how much you may pay over time based on the loan amount, interest rate, loan term, and payment frequency.

How to Use This Loan Calculator

Enter the loan amount, interest rate, repayment term, and payment frequency. The calculator will estimate the payment amount, total interest, total payments, and an amortization schedule.

What Is Amortization?

Amortization is the process of paying down a loan over time through regular payments. Early payments usually include more interest, while later payments reduce more principal.

Loan Payment Formula

The standard loan payment formula estimates a fixed payment based on principal, periodic interest rate, and total number of payments.

Example Calculation

If you borrow $100,000 for 10 years at a 6% annual interest rate with monthly payments, this calculator can estimate your monthly payment and total interest paid.
