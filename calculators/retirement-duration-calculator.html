What Is a Retirement Duration Calculator?

A retirement duration calculator estimates how long your retirement savings may last based on your monthly withdrawals and expected investment return.

How This Calculator Works

This calculator projects your balance over time by adding estimated investment growth and subtracting your monthly withdrawal.

Why Retirement Duration Matters

Knowing how long your savings may last can help you plan retirement spending and avoid withdrawing too much too quickly.

Example Calculation

If you have $500,000 saved, withdraw $3,000 per month, and earn a 5% annual return, this calculator estimates how long the money may last.
