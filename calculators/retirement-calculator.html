What Is a Retirement Calculator?

A retirement calculator estimates how much money you may need for retirement and compares that target with your projected savings.

How to Use This Retirement Calculator

Enter your current age, retirement age, income, savings, expected investment return, inflation rate, and estimated retirement income needs.

Why Retirement Planning Matters

Retirement planning helps estimate whether your current savings and future contributions may be enough to support your expected lifestyle after you stop working.

Retirement Savings Estimate
